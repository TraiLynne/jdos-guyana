History of J-Dos Internationalé, Inc.

JDOS Internationalé (Guyana), Inc. (JDOS) is a Guyanese woman owned and lead project management, construction, and consultant business. Founded in 1995, JDOS headquarters is in Washington, DC, with offices in Maryland. JDOS was established in Guyana in 2011 as the company expanded its services in Guyana being a Guyanese owned business. JDOS is committed to working with governments, commercial entities, NGOs and other institutions to achieve long term solutions to infrastructural challenges. JDOS has performed on several engineering design and construction projects in the Caribbean and Africa. Our results are built on tangible economic, environmental and social principles.

We and our teaming partners have over two (2) decades of relevant hands on experience managing and supporting some of the most prominent companies around the world the Americas, Europe, and Africa. JDOS has accumulated the requisite experience and expertise needed to conjoin with the local knowledge and expertise of Guyana. Our core values are fundamental to everything we do, including how we develop, engineer, and execute our projects.

JDOS understands the culture, norms, and reality of the local business environment in Guyana. We utilize local knowledge, resources and skills and our blended expertise of engineering, financial,legal, and project management project controls.

Our suite of supplementary services to government and private sector in the areas of financial, legal and environmental services can be accessed to satisfy the needs of a broad category of potential clients. Our commitment to our clients is to always approach all projects holistically, no matter the size or complexity.

Additionally, we identify the most sustainable and innovative solutions for all parties involved to ensure that our clients efficiently maximize the return on investment to their owners and shareholders.
