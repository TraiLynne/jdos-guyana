Our Services

Construction & Engineering Management

Construction and Engineering Management

Construction & Engineering Management

J-Dos manages projects from initial planning through to occupancy and operation. We use state-of –the-art technology, technical expertise and skilled management to deliver projects that meet the client’s goals and objectives on each project. We understand that projects today are more technical, and clients are demanding higher quality with tighter budgets for shorter time frames. We can meet those challenges. We have a proven history of successfully meeting the demands of this growing and ever changing industry for both new construction and renovation. Construction projects demand big-picture ideas and attention to details. We plan, coordinate, budget, and supervise all construction projects with the specific needs and goals of our clients at the forefront.

What Makes Us Unique

J-Dos applies the combined experiences of our staff in project planning, design management, construction management and inspection, commissioning and occupancy planning for sizable rehabilitation and renovations, new construction, and projects involving historic preservation to optimize time, cost and quality to our clients’ specific needs.

Our engineers maintain active professional licenses in the District of Columbia, Maryland, North Carolina, New York, Texas, and Virginia. Our construction inspectors possess the required certifications recognized by multiple State and Local agencies.

Each Project Team consists of the most capable and experienced individuals reporting to the Construction Manager who is responsible to the client’s management representative on the project. Staff members of the J-Dos team have managed and inspected design and construction projects exceeding $1 billion. The experience of our construction management staff includes the full gamut of pavement restoration, roadway reconstruction, streetscaping, and bridge rehabilitation projects. Our professionals have managed and inspected projects ranging from minor improvements projects to complex infrastructure development programs.

Corporate Services

JDos provides corporate services to support all of your corporate filing, formation and transaction needs from entity and deal management to service of process (SOP) and business licenses.

We receive and deliver important legal paperwork on our clients' behalf and assist them with compliance demands. We help source and maintain the right licenses and permits and related services that help companies expand their businesses into other states and other countries. Our corporate services include:

  • Corporate filings
  • Apostille, Authentication/Certification, Legalization
  • Registered Agent
  • Translation
  • Transaction

Corporate Filings

Formations:

Support in forming a corporation or limited liability structures.

Articles of Amendment:

Prepare and file all the certificates required to amend an entity's formation documents.

Dissolutions or Withdrawals:

Complete and file the appropriate state forms in instances where you must dissolve or withdraw a business entity.

Certificates of Good Standing:

Order Certificates of Good Standing for your entities from the Secretary of State in all 50 states and the District of Columbia—evidence that you have satisfied your annual report, franchise tax, and other obligations.

Document retrieval:

Order certified copies of any of your business documents that are filed with the Secretary of State, including formation documents.

Doing business as (DBA) filings:

Create a doing business as (DBA) filing history for entities at any jurisdictional level.

Foreign qualification:

Help companies qualify to do business in any state - researching the requirements of the state (or states) in question, determining costs, and providing you with the correct forms and filing guidelines.

Annual Report Services:

Prepare annual report forms on your behalf, file the required documents, and advance state fees directly to the Secretary of State.

Apostille, Authentication/ Certification, and Legalization:

Ordering apostille as validation of U.S. corporate documents in countries that are party to the Hague Convention. In countries that are not party to The Hague Convention, authentication and legalization of corporate documents can be conducted on behalf of our clients.

Registered Agent Services

  • Providing the required registered agent address for the states where you’re doing business.
  • Receiving and forwarding service of process and tax documents.
  • Sending email alerts to remind you when annual or periodic reports are due.

Translation Services

  • One-stop translation service with experienced multilingual and multidisciplinary team available to work on your translation projects in most major languages
  • Fast service delivery
